What is Reacquaint?
<br> Reacquaint is construction tender evaluation platform enabling bidders to upload tenders(PDF documents) and receive AI-driven assessments. The platform integrates AI to evaluate company and employee suitability, generating numerical scores across key criteria such as Budget Control, Environment, Health & Safety, Qualifications, and Quality. Authentication and session management were implemented to ensure secure bidding. PostgreSQL was utilized for efficient data handling, with optimized queries to enable fast retrieval of tender analytics.
